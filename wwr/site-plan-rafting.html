Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to facilitate enrollment and purchasing for customers and to provide information they need to book a trip. The website will benefit our company by increasing sales, cutomer satisfaction, and advertising.

Audience

Our target audience is individuals and families seeking to explore the outdoors while experiencing the intense power of nature. We are seeking to attract individuals who are outdoorsy, athletic, and adventurous. They will access my site on desktop, laptop, and mobile device. The content, colors, fonts, etc. will be carefully chosen to be visiually inviting to this kind of person.
