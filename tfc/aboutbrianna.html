So, who is Brianna?

Brianna is a newleywed wife and soon-to-be mom. She is passionate about the culinary arts and has big plans for the future. Read more about her below.

❮ ❯

Beginnings

Brianna started piano lessons at the young age of three. She played for five years and was a skilled pianist. As she grew, her interested shifted to marital arts. Her father and several other family members participated in Taekwondo with Brianna. She received her first-degree black belt in 2016.

Present-Day

Brianna's interest in culinary arts and baking started in 2018. She taught herself to bake and discovered a new love. Her passion for creating food and desserts led her to pursue a career in culinary arts. She continued to work on pastries and food at home until 2021. She moved to Utah and began her first culinary job.

Looking Into the Future

Since then, Brianna has moved back to California and furthered her experience at other restaurants. She has married and is a soon-to-be mother. Her growing family drives her to continue to pursue her culinary dreams. Brianna plans to have a themed restaurant that offers a new twist to the food her ancestors made.
