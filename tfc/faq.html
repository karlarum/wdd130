General FAQ'S

We are not ready to open but are working towards opening in the coming year. We are putting together a menu and creating a wide range of options for customers to try. We hope you continue to check in!

The Flavor Club will be offering catering shortly after opening. Options will include both entrees and desserts, as seen in the menu. More information will be available in the near future.

The Flavor Club offers baked goods that are dairy-free. We are not currently selling baked goods, but they will be available for purchase or catering upon request.

The Flavor Club desserts contain common allergens. Allergen lists are found below the product descriptions. We are open to creating desserts without common allergens! We will have an allergen free desert menu for those that would like it. Feel free to message us with any questions or requests.

A few reviews are available on the home page. We are working on collecting more reviews and posting them on the website. We strive to remain honest and are open to critique.

This site will be used for Brianna to showcase her previous work and any projects she is currently working on. She has culinary expertise in both baking and cooking gourmet meals. As she works to perfect her menu, she will update prospective customers or employers through her online portfolio.

Visitors will need to visit this site because it is the only site where they can contact Brianna directly with any catering requests, job offers, or general questions. She does not respond as quickly via social media messaging. This site will send all inquiries directly to her email. Visitors should expect to hear a response within 72 hours

Any visitors interested in receiving updates will have the option to enter an email address. They should also have the option to message Brianna through the site. These messages should go straight to her email.

FAQ'S About Chef Brianna

Brianna has the following certifications: Food Handlers Basic Safety Course and Food Handlers COVID-19 Training.

Brianna has worked at several different restaurants. She has worked at Stanza Italian Bistro & Wine Bar, Costa Vida, and CHAAK.
